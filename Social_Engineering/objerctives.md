# objectives

+ You will have a solid understanding of what Social Engineering is and how it is leveraged by attackers for initial access.
+ You will have an understanding of various Social Engineering techniques, how they work and when they are used.
+ You will have a solid understanding of what Phishing and Spear phishing attacks are, how they work and when they are used.
+ You will have an understanding of what Pretexting is and the role it plays in a successful Social Engineering campaign.
+ You will have the ability to plan and orchestrate a Phishing campaign and setup the required infrastructure to facilitate the campaign.
+ You will be able to plan, setup and orchestrate a Phishing campaign with an open source Phishing framework like Gophish
